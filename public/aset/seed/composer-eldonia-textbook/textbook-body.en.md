## Introduction — What is Eldonia-Nex?

Eldonia-Nex is a creative economy where creators and fans connect through works, commerce, and collaboration. Publish in GALLERY, co-create in Lab, and expand into SHOP, EVENTS, and WORKS.

Under a black-and-gold Nexus identity, illustration, manga, photo, music, and story coexist. This textbook walks new visitors through the UI chapter by chapter.

## Chapter 1 — Account and basics

Sign up from the home page, confirm your email, then open Settings → Basics to set display name, username, and bio.

Turn on “Act as a creator” if you plan to post works, sell in SHOP, or join Lab collabs. Notification preferences are also managed in Settings.

## Chapter 2 — GALLERY — Enjoying works

GALLERY lists illustrations, manga, photos, music, and stories. Use realm filters for manga, photo, story, and more. [sfx:page-turn|Turn a page]

Each work opens in a tailored viewer: vertical manga scroll, photo slideshow, narrated story reader, or audio player. Like, comment, and follow creators you enjoy.

## Chapter 3 — Posting your work

Go to Settings → Post artwork to publish to GALLERY. Images, video, audio, and PDF are supported; manga and photos can be multi-page.

For story works, add a PDF, synopsis, and chapter-style body text (## headings) for the narrated reader. Inline [sfx:…] markers for sound effects are planned for a future update.

## Chapter 4 — Lab — Co-creation rooms

Send a collab request from a work you like. When the author accepts, a Lab room opens for member file sharing and progress (features expanding).

Gallery → Lab is the core flow: team up from a work and ship results back to GALLERY, SHOP, or events.

## Chapter 5 — SHOP, EVENTS, WORKS

Browse and buy in SHOP. Discover online or in-person plans in EVENTS. WORKS offers Quest-style listings and portfolio ties.

All paths connect back to GALLERY activity and Lab collaboration.

## Chapter 6 — Community and support

COMMUNITY offers board-style discussion. Check Help → FAQ first; open a support ticket if needed. Logged-in users can follow up in My Tickets.

## Closing — Your next step

After this textbook, explore other demos in GALLERY (10-page manga, fantasy photo album). Your own posts and Lab participation enrich the Nexus.

Welcome to the Nexus—Eldonia-Nex supports your creative journey.
